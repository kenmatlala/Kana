Security for Real People

KANA is built for a world that never stops pulling for our attention. While the WEB takes from us, tracks us, and tempts us, we create tools that protect, quiet, and give control back to people. We guard privacy, reduce digital harm, help break unhealthy online habits, and keep children safe from things they shouldn't have to see. KANA is not security for systems or companies – it is security for real people, living real lives, online.

ABOUT US

KANA began with a conviction to protect what matters.

What started as an effort to shield ourselves from powerful companies harvesting our data became something deeper. Through prayer and conviction, we were led to build tools that help people struggling with pornography — not with condemnation, but with truth, accountability, and grace. From there, the calling expanded to protecting children and families in an increasingly hostile digital world.

We believe technology should serve people, not enslave them.

KANA builds free digital tools that help individuals guard their privacy, break harmful online patterns, and protect children from content that damages hearts and minds. Our work is rooted in faith, guided by responsibility, and driven by love for people.
